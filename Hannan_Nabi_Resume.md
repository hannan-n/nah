## Projects

**Scalar autodiff engine.** Python. Roughly 300 lines.

A scalar-valued autograd built from scratch, with manual backpropagation over a DAG of operations. Written to understand how PyTorch and JAX actually work, not to replace them. [github.com/hannan-n/autodiff](https://github.com/hannan-n/autodiff)

**Bayesian LSTM for volatility forecasting.** PyTorch. In progress.

A small hybrid model: GARCH for the conditional variance structure, an LSTM for the residual dynamics, and variational inference over the LSTM weights for posterior uncertainty. The interesting question is whether the posterior is well-calibrated out of sample.

**Interactive SVD lab.** WebGL.

A 2x2 singular value decomposition explorer. You change the matrix, you see the singular values, the condition number, the rank, and the unit circle being mapped. Lives as the hidden puzzle on my site.
